Check out The Improv Centre’s hilarious improv shows!

Doors open one hour before showtime.
Shows are 90 minutes long and include a 15-minute intermission, unless otherwise specified. 

Theatresports™

Fridays & Saturdays at 

7:30pm | PG – Some material may not be suitable for some audiences.
&
9:30pm | 14A – May include suggestive language and/or scenes. 

Two teams of highly skilled improv comedians vie for the laughs and support of audience judges. A high-energy, fast-paced and good-natured competition that will leave you wanting more.

It’s the show we based our whole performance style on for 40 years, so you know the comedy in this show is top notch!
