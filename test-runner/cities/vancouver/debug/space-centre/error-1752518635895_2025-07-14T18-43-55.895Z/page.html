Community Group Visits

If you’re looking for a fun and educational outing as a group, you’ve come to the right place! Whether you are a group of friends, coworkers, or even residents of a senior centre, we are happy to welcome you to explore the wonders of space.

We offer group pricing for visitors attending our regular daytime or evening programming. These visits are self-guided, and include time in the Cosmic Courtyard gallery, a live science demonstration, and a show in the planetarium.

Planetarium Star Theatre 
Experience the only full-dome, digital projection system in Vancouver! Journey through new discoveries in our universe with our ever changing Planetarium shows. 

GroundStation Canada Theatre
Our live science demonstration theatre offers a unique experience on topics like living and travelling in space, rocketry, and the science of stars. 

Cosmic Courtyard Gallery 
Immerse yourself in our hands-on, space-themed gallery, where you can morph into an alien, and touch an authentic Moon rock or meteorite.

Add a Workshop for an Interactive Experience

Your group can add on a workshop or a customizable observatory tour for an extra fee to make your visit even more fun and interesting. 

Rocket Science 101
This hands-on workshop gives you and your group a chance to discover more about what makes a rocket fly. Plus, launch your own rockets!
(Program length approximately 60 minutes and is recommended for ages 8+).

Observatory Visit
Discover why astronomers are passionate about the stars. Join us for a special visit to our Gordon Southam Observatory where your group can use our telescopes and have questions answered about the universe around them. (Program length approximately 30 minutes.)

Cosmic Birthday Parties

Give your child a unique birthday that’s out of this world. Host their party at the Space Centre!

Choose one of our birthday party packages and enjoy a show in the planetarium and take part in other space themed activities with your group.

Programs last approximately 3 hours.

Packages:

Mission to the Moon (Age 7+)
Do you and your friends want to visit the Moon? Head off to the Moon for your birthday! Learn what it takes to live in space so when you ‘land’ on the Moon you are ready for adventure. NOTE: This is an experience better suited for younger children but has enough depth if older children are particularly keen on the topic.

Includes:
– Forward to the Moon show in the planetarium 
– Day in Space live demonstration in the GroundStation Canada Theatre
– Mission patch activity in Cosmic Courtyard gallery
Time for opening presents will also be included.

Rocket to the Moon (Age 10+)
Have you ever wanted to know what it takes to be an astronaut? Head off to the Moon for your birthday! You will build and launch your own rockets, and explore the Moon and its surrounding neighbourhood. NOTE: This is not an experience suitable for children under 10 years as it involves using hot glue guns.

Includes:
– Forward to the Moon show in the planetarium show
– Rocket construction workshop and launch
Time for opening presents will also be included.

Timing:
This program is booked in the late afternoon for 3:30pm. The program lasts approximately three hours.

***Program will not begin until all members of your group are present. Please arrive by 3:30pm***

Price: $42.75 per child plus applicable taxes. Adults $21.75 each (Space Centre members save 20% on birthday party bookings).

Group Size:
A minimum group size of 15 children can be accommodated in this program, and a maximum of 30 children and adults.

A $90.00 maintenance fee (general room preparation, furniture set-up and take-down, and cleaning) applies to all birthday parties in addition to attendee fees.

Booking Changes/Cancellation Policy
Deposit: A non-refundable deposit of 20% is required upon booking. The deposit secures your booking and will be applied to the total cost. The deposit will be kept if a cancellation occurs.

Birthday Party FAQ's

What should I do when I arrive?

You can check in at the front desk when you arrive and have your guests assemble in the main lobby. Guest services will be there to assist you with present drop-off, directions, and payment. 

What happens if some of my guests arrive late?

Ensure that all members of your group arrive on time. Please let the guest services staff know if you expect late guests and we will connect them with the rest of the party when they arrive.

Do we receive a guided tour?

This is a guided birthday party. Our interpretive staff will provide full programming for your group over a three-hour period.

Can I bring my own room decorations?

Table decorations are acceptable.

We ask that you not bring any other decorations. The birthday party will take place in our exhibit and theatre facilities which are fully decorated in a space theme.

What if the number of guests changes on the day of the party?

We collect payment on the day of the party, so you will only pay for the number of people who show up (based on the minimum of 15 people).

What if I need to cancel a birthday party?

Please be advised that cancellations made within less than one week of the party are subject to a $50 cancellation fee. However, if the cancellation is due to COVID-19, we will make accommodations.

Overnight Adventures

Book your group for an Overnight Adventure and sleepover at the Space Centre! Learn about the night sky in the planetarium, catch a live science demonstration, and build a bottle rocket. In the morning a light breakfast is served followed by rocket launching.

This program is for children in grades 4 to 7, or the ages of 7 to 14 for community groups (eg. Scouts and Guides). Children must be accompanied by adult chaperones for the entire program.

Programming and activities run for approximately 3 hours in total.

Program Includes
– an overnight stay at the Space Centre
– time to explore our Cosmic Courtyard exhibit space
– a live science demonstration
– a hands-on rocket building workshop and launch
– a tour of the observatory
– an orientation to the night sky in the planetarium star theatre
– evening snack and light breakfast

Schedule

  • 8:00pm – Arrival
  • 8:15pm – Cosmic Courtyard and Groundstation Canada Show
  • 9:00pm – Rocket Building and Observatory Tour
  • 10:15pm – Snack and set up for bedtime
  • 10:45pm – Planetarium Show 
  • 11:30pm Bedtime

———

  • 7:30am – Wake-up
  • 8:00am – Breakfast
  • 8:30am – Rocket Launch
  • 9:00am – Departure

Group Size
A minimum group size of 25 can be accommodated in this program, maximum of 60 including chaperones.

Age Range: 7 – 14 (check out our Not-Quite-An-Overnight program for younger children)

Program availability: Any night of the week (Note we are also open to the public on Wednesday and Friday evenings).

Program run time: 8:00pm – 9:00am
