Notice: Canada Post and possible service disruption

Due to a potential service disruption with Canada Post, we are currently only mailing tickets for performances scheduled more than 21 days from the date of purchase.

For all other performances, you may choose to receive digital tickets or select Will-Call pickup at the Box Offices. Tickets can be picked up during regular business hours or at Will-Call on the day of your performance.

Alternatively, you can access your tickets through your Mirvish Digital Wallet — no additional app download is required.

For more information, please call us at 1.800.461.3333 or visit us online.
