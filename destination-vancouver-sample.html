Don’t miss these moments

Where the action happens

BC Place

Home to the White Caps (MLS) and BC Lions (CFL), attend Vancouver’s largest events, from sports to concerts under a uniquely retractable roof. Experience the roar of the crowd in this spectacular venue, rain or shine. 

Rogers Arena

Head on over to this arena for a lively indoor entertainment scene that is home to the Vancouver Canucks NHL and the Vancouver Warriors. As a more intimate venue, you can step right into the action.

Queen Elizabeth Theatre

Indulge in the exquisite performances of opera, ballet, and Broadway shows in this grand auditorium where artistry and passion come to life on stage.

Vancouver Aquarium

Immerse yourself in the marine world of diverse aquatic creatures, interactive exhibits, and conservation efforts.
